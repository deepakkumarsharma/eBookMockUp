So, how to use this book

Zoom to have a closer look

You have many options to zoom the pages in or out. You may zoom the pages in our out by just single or double mouse tap. The arrows at the upper right corner also serves zooming in and out. Explore the content of the book by dragging (click and hold left mouse button and move) the zoomed pages right and left or up and down. ESC key escapes you from the zoomed view.

Flip pages to read more

Flip to the next or the previous page of the book, by moving the way we turn page of a physical book or by clicking the upper or bottom extreme corners of the book. Hovering a mouse on the right or left sides of the book, will allow you to use arrows provided to flip the pages. You may also enjoy reading by simply using the left and right navigation keys.

Visit other chapter

No need to turn the pages to jump to another chapter. Just click on the numbers above the book and go to desired chapter directly.
